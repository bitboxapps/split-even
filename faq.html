SplitEven makes splitting bills easy in just a few steps: Take a photo of your receipt, and our AI automatically reads all the items and prices. Then add the friends who are splitting the bill, tap to assign items to each person, and SplitEven calculates exactly what everyone owes including their fair share of tax and tip.

SplitEven works with most printed receipts from restaurants, cafes, bars, and retail stores. For best results, make sure the receipt is flat, well-lit, and the text is clearly visible. The app supports receipts in multiple languages and currencies.
Tax and tip are split proportionally based on the total of items each person is paying for. For example, if you ordered $30 worth of food and your friend ordered $20, you would pay 60% of the tax and tip while your friend pays 40%. This ensures everyone pays their fair share.
